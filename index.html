Instructions to sell your item:

  1. Fill in and submit the form.
  2. Your item will be listed here.
  3. The buyer will contact you and start the negotiation. Negotion includes whether or not to use our delivery service.
    Using the $1 delivery service:
  1. Once a deal is made, the buyer is required to fill in the delivery form and pay the delivery fee as well as the amount of the item to us.
  2. We will contact you to confirm the location that we can pick up your item as well as return your money.
  3. We deliver your item to the buyer.
  4. Congratulations! You have made an income via our platform.
    Not using the delivery service:
  1. Settle the transaction between you and your buyer.
  2. Once a deal is made, inform us about that so that we can delist your item.

FAQ:

  1. How much is the transaction fee?

    We do not charge any fee on transaction. We only charge $1 on using our delivery service.

  2. How do you deliver my item?

    Once the deal is made, we will collect your item in any location in the NTU campus between 12pm and 7pm.

  3. What can I sell?

    You can sell new items, second-hand items or hand-made items on the platform. Only items that are legally approve by the law of Singapore can be sold. We retain the right to delist your item if we discover that the item is not promoting social good.

  4. Can I sell refrigerator?

    You can sell refrigerator. However, we do not provide delivery service on any item which is greater than 5kg. You may consider to deliver yourself.

  5. I am not a NTU student, can I sell on your platform?

    Can, but we will only collect the item within the NTU campus.
